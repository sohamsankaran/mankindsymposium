About

A new optimism pervades the 21st century born of a generation that lives in a world that is no longer bound by geographical borders or physical walls. By giving people the right to determine their lives and debate their role in society, ordinary people, rational people can play a part in charting the history of the future. By bringing together the experiences of the experienced and the unique life experiences of the young generation, the Mankind Symposium will scrutinize the acts of nations that have been romanticized and idealized and recognize that human beings everywhere have the same rights.
